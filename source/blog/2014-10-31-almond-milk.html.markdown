READMORE

---

## Ingredient 

* 100g of almond
* 1L of water
* 2 dessert spoon of honey 

## Preparation

1. Let the almond one night in water 
2. Wring the almond
3. mix the almond, the water and the honey
5. Grind it
6. Filter
7. Put in the fridge and enjoy
