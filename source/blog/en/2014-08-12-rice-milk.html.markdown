## Ingredient

* 40g of rice
* 1,340 L of water
* 3 dessert spoon of honey or 3 dates

## Preparation

1. Let the rice one night in water 
2. Wring the rice
3. Put in pan the rice, the water and the honey
4. Cook it for 10 min, let rest 15 min
5. Grind it
6. Filter
7. Put in the fridge and enjoy
